💌 An Open Letter to You, Sayang

My Sayang, I don’t really know how to start this perfectly, so I will just speak from my heart. If you are reading this, I just want you to know that you mean more to me than I can ever explain with words. You are not just someone I love. You are someone who became part of my thoughts, my daily feelings, and my quiet moments. I think about you when I wake up. I think about you when I’m busy. And I think about you when everything becomes quiet at night. Sometimes I don’t express myself well. Sometimes I make mistakes. Sometimes I get scared of losing you and I react in the wrong way. But even in all of that… My feelings for you never change. I don’t love you because life is easy. I love you because you are important to me even when life is hard. I love the way you care. I love the way you try. I love even the small things you do that you don’t even notice yourself. And I want you to know something very important: You don’t have to be perfect for me. You just have to be you. If I ever make you feel tired, I’m sorry. If I ever make you feel confused, I’m sorry. But please know I am always trying to be better for you. Not because I’m forced. But because I care. I don’t know what our future will look like. But I know one thing clearly in my heart: If love is a choice… I will choose you again and again. Even in my weakest days. Even in my hardest moments. Even when I don’t know what to say. Because you are my peace. And I don’t want to lose that. Always yours, Your sayang 💌

— forever trying, forever yours
